This example demonstrates different unicode-bidi.Change the unicode-bidi of text by clicking on the different unicode-bidi of text to see what happend!
the code:
div {
direction:rtl;
unicode-bidi:initial;
}
syntax:unicode-bidi: normal|embed|bidi-override|intitial|inherit;

Let's play with text unicode-bidi!

unicode-bidi
normal normal Does not use an additional level of embedding. This is default.
embed embed Creates an additional level of embedding.
bidi-override bidi-override Creates an additional level of embedding. Reordering depends on the direction property.
initial initial Sets this property to its default value.
