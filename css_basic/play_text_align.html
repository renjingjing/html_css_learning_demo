This example demonstrates different text aligns. Everywhere you look you find games, games and more games. They are on your cell phone, connected to your TV via a gaming console and everywhere you look on the web. People love their games, even the corny ones.
the code:
div {
text-align:left;
}

Let's play with text align!

left Aligns the text to the left.
right Aligns the text to the right.
center Centers the text.
justify Stretches the lines so that each line has equal width (like in newspapers and magazines.
initial Sets this property to its default value.
